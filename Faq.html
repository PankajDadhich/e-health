Frequently Asked Questions

What are the Visas available?

EB3, H1B and TN Visas

Can I choose a state or hospital?

Currently, specific job selection is not available. We partner with top hospitals across the U.S.

How many hours per week will I work?

Typically 36 hours/week: 3 days of 12-hour shifts. Confirm during your interview.

Do you accept applicants globally?

Yes, except countries on the US watch list or under travel ban.

What are your requirements?

RN: NCLEX or 3+ yrs acute care. MT: ASCPi & 3+ yrs exp. Others: US licensure required.

Do you have flight guidelines?

Yes, including luggage limits and a pre-flight briefing. Welcome packet also provided.

Will you take deployment photos?

Only with your consent. We respect your privacy and never post without permission.

Can I ask immigration questions?

Yes, but no legal advice. Consult an attorney for complex visa matters.

Do you follow GDPR?

Yes, we respect privacy. No data is shared with third parties without consent.

Do OPNYSED accept debit cards?

Please check their official website for the latest payment options.

Can EHJ process school documents?

We do not process documents abroad. You may request help if near a global office.

Is there an online portal?

Yes, for document uploads and step-by-step guidance through the deployment process.

Where can I find testimonials?

We rely on word of mouth, but you may find mentions of us on social platforms.

Will EHJ pick me up at the airport?

Yes, a licensed chauffeur will greet and escort you to your accommodation.

Are you a staffing agency?

No. We only offer direct hire placements to our partner hospitals in the United States.
