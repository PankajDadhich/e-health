Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What are the Visas available?

Ans

EB3, H1B and TN Visas

Q

Are you a staffing agency?

Ans

No. We only offer direct hire placements to our partner hospitals in the United States.

Q

Can I choose a state or hospital where to work in?

Ans

At this time, the option to choose specific job openings is not available, as we have carefully curated opportunities for you. We are partnering with leading hospitals across the U.S., ensuring access to high-quality positions.

Q

How many hours per week am I going to work?

Ans

You will be working 36 hours per week, typically 3 days of 12 hours as per your agreed upon schedule. You should be asking this question during your interview process.

Q

Do you accept applicants from specific countries only?

Ans

We accept applicants globally, except for those countries on the US watch list, or if the US has a travel ban from your country.

Q

What are your requirements for applying?

Ans

For RNs, you must have already passed the NCLEX. Non-NCLEX passers may still apply if you have 3+ years recent experience in acute care bedside. You will still have to pass the NCLEX in order to start a job in the United States. For MTs, ASCPi and VisaScreen Certification but minimum 3+ years experience required. For Other Allied Healthcare providers (Rad Tech, PT, OT, SLP, etc.) – US licensure in your specific field.

Q

Do you have a welcome packet or flight guidelines during deployment?

Ans

We do have flight guidelines for you to follow, and a 2 luggage limit per person . We have a pre-flight deployment meeting to tell you everything that will happen along your journey. We also deliver a welcome packet with detailed instructions how to start your first few weeks in the US successfully. For certain candidates we may escort you personally to the social security office and set up bank account, etc. It all depends on who you get a job with.

Q

Does EHJ take a photo during deployment for marketing purposes?

Ans

Typically, we do not take photos unless you want to take them yourself and share them with us. We respect your privacy and understand not everyone wants their pictures on platforms for the world to see and also to know your business. We have not advertise not even once is almost 2 decades, but we do rely on word of mouth. If you would like to take your own photos and share with us, we would post it only with your permission.

Q

Can I ask you questions about immigration?

Ans

You can ask us since it is our job to know all the immigration steps you need to get through to arrive in the US, however, we will not answer anything that can be construed as legal advice. You will have to ask an attorney for advice on Visas for anyone other than yourself. You should not expect your recruiter to help you in a legal sense.

Q

Does EHJ have General Data Protection Regulations - Consent Form for Candidates to sign?

Ans

We obey all website privacy laws and by using this website you automatically give consent to be contacted by us during the normal course of business. We do not rent or share any of your personal information with any other 3rd party company, nor do we ask you to share any personal information you do not wish to. Should you wish to work with us, a resume is required, and it will have certain identifying information. You may redact anything you want until you trust us, but we need enough info to decide you are a fit for our jobs and to contact you.

Q

Do OPNYSED they accept debit cards?

Ans

You may want to go to their website and check with them as to whether or not they will accept your debit card. We cannot speak for them and their rules can change anytime.

Q

If the nurses are outside the country can EHJ process on their behalf school documents and stateboard verification?

Ans

Since all of our nurses are outside the United States, we do not send a representative to your country to process your school documents. If your school happens to be close to one of our global offices, you may ask your recruiter for a favor but we cannot guarantee they will do this for everyone.

Q

Does EHJ have an online portal to log in?

Ans

Yes. We have a private login and portal for our candidates to create an account, upload their immigration documents and be guided through all their steps. The portal has many articles to learn from and also has a step by step guide to the deployment process so all your questions are answered in one place.

Q

Is there a platform or website where I can see people's testimony?

Ans

We do not keep a platform just for testimonials, but there may be people talking about us on several platforms. We do not have a Facebook account, so if you find us on Facebook it's because someone went there to ask about us or talk about us.

Q

Will EHJ pick me up at the airport or only a representative?

Ans

Airport pickup is done by a licensed and bonded professional chauffer. You will know his name ahead of time and he will make sure you get to your temporary accomodations quickly and in comfort. We are very proud of the type of people we use, and everyone who has met them has felt happy with their experience..
