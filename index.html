TN Visa for Nurses

If you are a Registered Nurses from Canada and Mexico, can expect to arrive in the US as soon as 2-4 months if you have passed the NCLEX and English exam.

H1b for Allied Health Workers

Our Med Techs, PTs, OTs, SLPs, Social Workers, Psychologists, Pharmacists etc can come to the US within 3-6 months depending on several factors.

H1b for Nurses

A newer pathway for international RNs to come to the US is an H1b Visa. Our hospitals and attorneys have successfully helped nurses with BSN and advanced certs to arrive in 4 -6 months.

EB3 or Diversity Visa

We have tons of jobs for CNAs, Radiology Techs, Physician Assistants, and other areas in a hospital and we help you successfully come to the United States with a green card in hand!

Nurse minimum requirements:

  • BSN in Nursing with min 2 years recent bedside experience
  • Passed NCLEX Exam
  • Passed IELTS or TOEFL Exam or OET (English Exam)
  • BLS Certification
  • Contract 3-4 years with employer who sponsors you
  • Pass international background and employment checks
  • Credentialed by CGFNS VisaScreen or Visa4Nurses
  • Pass a medical exam and drug test within 30 days of arrival
  • Covid Vaccination and Negative Covid Test

Med Tech minimum requirements:

  • Bachelor Degree
  • 2-3 years of hospital, reference or diagnostic lab experience
  • Must have ASCPi
  • Passed IELTS or TOEFL Exam or OET (English Exam)
  • Contract 3-4 years with employer who sponsors you
  • Pass international background and employment checks
  • Credentialed by CGFNS VisaScreen
  • Pass a medical exam and drug test within 30 days of arrival
  • Covid Vaccination and Negative Covid Test
